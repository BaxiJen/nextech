Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

BaXiJen Tecnologia Ltda., a Brazilian private legal entity, registered under CNPJ nº 54.876.543/0001-22, headquartered in Rio de Janeiro/RJ, hereafter referred to as "BaXiJen", is the data controller of personal data processed through its institutional website (baxijen.com.br), the BXat platform, and AI agent and WhatsApp chatbot services.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect personal data from users of our services, in compliance with the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD — Law nº 13.709/2018) and other applicable legislation.

1. Data we collect

1.1 Data provided by the user

  • Account data: name, email, phone, and CNPJ provided via contact forms or platform registration.
  • Access credentials: encrypted email and password for platform authentication.
  • Conversation content: messages exchanged with AI agents (text, images, audio, documents) when the service is in use.
  • Integration data: connection information for WhatsApp Business API, CRMs, spreadsheets, and other integrated systems.

1.2 Automatically collected data

  • Browsing data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and time spent.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: used for session maintenance, usage analysis, and experience improvement (see section 7).
  • Device data: model, operating system, and identifiers of the device used to access the services.

1.3 Corporate client data

  • Business information (CNPJ, company name, commercial contacts) provided during the hiring process.
  • Platform usage data, including service metrics, message volume, and agent configurations.

2. How we use data

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service provision: processing and responding to messages, managing conversations, and providing AI-powered service on contracted channels.
  • Account management: authentication, profile configuration, and platform administration.
  • System integration: connecting with WhatsApp Business API, CRMs, spreadsheets, and other systems authorized by the client.
  • Service improvement: aggregated and anonymized usage analysis to enhance features and train AI models.
  • Communication: sending service notifications, updates, technical support, and responses to inquiries.
  • Legal compliance: meeting legal, regulatory, and competent authority requirements.

3. Legal bases for processing

Personal data processing is based on the following legal grounds (Art. 7, LGPD):

  • Contract execution: for providing contracted services.
  • Consent: when the user voluntarily provides data via forms or initiates a conversation with AI agents.
  • Legitimate interest: for platform improvement, security, and continuity.
  • Legal obligation: for compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Data sovereignty

Personal data processed by BaXiJen is stored and processed on Brazilian infrastructure. No sensitive data leaves the national territory. Data sovereignty is an architectural principle, not merely a contractual promise.

5. Data sharing

Data may be shared with the following third parties, exclusively for service provision:

  • Meta Platforms (WhatsApp Business API): for sending and receiving WhatsApp messages, as authorized by the client.
  • Infrastructure providers: hosting and processing services located in Brazil.
  • AI model providers: for natural language processing and response generation, with anonymized data where possible.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

6. International data transfer

Any international transfers are made in compliance with Art. 33 of the LGPD, ensuring an adequate level of protection through contractual clauses and technical measures. Where possible, we use providers with infrastructure in Brazil.

7. Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain user session authentication.
  • Analyze platform usage (Google Analytics).
  • Improve browsing experience.

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. Cookies essential for service operation cannot be disabled.

8. Data security

We adopt technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.
  • Passwords stored with cryptographic hash (bcrypt).
  • JWT token authentication.
  • Role-based access control (RBAC).
  • Access monitoring and logging.
  • Regular encrypted backups.
  • Automatic masking of sensitive data (CPF, CNPJ) in logs.

9. Data retention

Personal data is retained for as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal obligations. After account closure, data is deleted or anonymized within 90 days, unless legal retention requires otherwise.

10. Data subject rights

In compliance with the LGPD, you have the right to:

  • Confirmation of the existence of data processing.
  • Access to processed personal data.
  • Correction of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data.
  • Anonymization, blocking, or deletion of unnecessary data.
  • Data portability.
  • Deletion of data processed with consent.
  • Revocation of consent.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: contato@baxi.ia.br
