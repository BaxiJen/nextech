Privacy Policy — BXat

Last updated: April 23, 2026

BaXiJen ("we", "our") operates the BXat platform and related services. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: name, email address, and profile picture (when provided via social login).
  • Usage data: conversations with AI models, configuration preferences, and access logs.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and diagnostic data.

2. How we use your information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our AI services.
  • Authenticate your access to the platform.
  • Send relevant communications about the service.
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse.

3. AI processing

Your conversations are processed by AI models hosted on our own infrastructure (on-premise). We do not use your conversations to train AI models. Conversation data is stored so you can access your history.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • When required to comply with legal obligations.
  • With authentication providers (Google, Apple) solely for login purposes.
  • In case of corporate reorganization, with prior notice.

5. Storage and security

Your data is stored on servers located in Brazil, protected by encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. We adopt appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

6. Your rights (LGPD)

In compliance with Brazil's General Data Protection Law (Law No. 13,709/2018), you may:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Request data portability.
  • Revoke consent at any time.
  • Request anonymization or blocking of unnecessary data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at contato@baxijen.ai.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and platform operation. We do not use advertising tracking cookies.

8. Data retention

We retain your data while your account is active. Upon deletion request, your data will be removed within 30 days, unless legally required to retain it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notice.
